the build

i started with finding the right vehicle, at first i wanted to use my truck though i quickly realised that i wouldn't have the space i wanted for this project. so i quickly set upon finding a van of some kind, i fixated upon finding a "bread-box" style van before realising that that style is called a step van. finding out that step vans range from $4,000 to $40,000 depending on features and year.
